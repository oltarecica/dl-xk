## Term Poster

### Research Question

- Any topic is allowed.
- Must include at least one prediction problem.
- Econometric models may be combined with deep learning models when causal interpretation is required.

### Grading Criteria

| Criteria | Details |
|----------|----------|
| Question | Substantive research question |
| Background | Review of related papers |
| Model | Model choice and approach |
| Data | Collection, construction, cleaning |
| Estimation | Optimisation, tuning, statistics |
| Results | Interpretation and answer |
| Writing | Clarity, organisation, references |

### Poster Requirements

Include:

1. Introduction
2. Research question
3. Background literature
4. Data
5. Methodology
6. Results and interpretation
7. Future research directions

Maximum length: **1500 words** (excluding captions, tables, references, appendix, and code).

### Tips

- Focus on a substantive research question.
- Prioritise an appropriate methodology over strong results.
- Select models relevant to the question.
- Keep all content tied to the research question.
- Consider dimensionality challenges when using image data.
- Avoid off-the-shelf Kaggle datasets unless directly relevant.
- Use LLMs as learning tools, not as a substitute for understanding.
